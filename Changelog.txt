05.06.2007 - jsPgnViewer/WP plugin 0.3.8
Change(s)
	3 different versions:
		+ (DEFAULT) packed with Packer (http://dean.edwards.name/packer/)
		+ packed with JSMin (http://www.crockford.com/javascript/jsmin.html)
		+ unpacked
